Each colour tells a story of transformation. Explore our collections below, sorted by the plastic they were born from, and discover the unique beauty hidden within discarded waste.

The Orange Collection

From a powdered orange juice container found washed up on the beach to empty lotion bottles from local households, this vibrant collection is a splash of Maldivian sunshine. The bright, warm tones are perfect for statement pieces that carry a story of rescue and reuse.

The Blue Collection

This collection is born from the lids of a popular drinking water brand found across our beaches. Each pair of earrings uses three bottletops, turning the most common form of plastic pollution into a beautiful reminder of our deep blue ocean and the life we are fighting to protect.

The Pink Collection

Crafted from laundry detergent bottles saved from being burned, this collection is a testament to direct action. One bottle creates around 150g of usable material. The soft pink, mixed with white flakes, creates a delicate, marbled effect, turning household waste into wearable art.

The Green Collection

Green is our most used color, sourced from the bright green lids of Taza water bottles and lotion containers found on every corner of our island. This collection is inspired by the lush palm trees and vibrant life of the Maldives, transforming everyday waste into a piece of paradise.

The White Collection

White plastic, collected from cosmetic and shampoo bottles, is a vital part of our process. It forms the canvas for creating beautiful marble effects. We rarely make pure white items; instead, we use it to add depth and artistry, decorating with our offcut "confetti" to ensure nothing goes to waste.

Confetti Keychain

Truly One of a Kind

Because of our manual crafting process, every single item we produce is unique. The colours mix in slightly different ways each time, meaning the piece you receive will be yours and yours alone—a true one-of-a-kind creation.

Custom Designs
