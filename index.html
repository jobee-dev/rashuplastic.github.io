Our Mission

Our mission is to be the first local island in the Maldives that recycles 100% of our plastic waste.

Environmental Impact

Launch the inaugural recycling initiative on our island to minimise plastic pollution, reduce waste burning, and protect our precious marine ecosystem.

Circular Economy

By transforming pollution into meaningful, handcrafted products, we promote resource reuse, benefiting both the environment and local communities.

Community Empowerment

Empower islanders, schoolchildren, and visitors through educational training on recycling and advocating for the health of our oceans.

A Plastic Problem in Paradise

The Maldives faces an urgent and growing challenge from plastic pollution, threatening people's health, the marine ecosystem and tourism. A significant portion of waste originates from items such as plastic drinking bottles, cosmetics and food packaging, while ocean currents wash in even more from overseas.

Waste management on local islands is limited, plastic is usually openly burned as a “quick fix".
