Our Solution: From Waste to Wonder

Every product we create is a piece of pollution removed from our beaches and oceans. Discover the journey of how we give plastic a new life.

From Ocean Waste to Ocean Wonder

Watch as discarded plastic bottle caps are reborn into beautiful, meaningful creations through our 5-step transformation process.

1

Step 1

Beach Collection

Every journey begins with a single step. Our community volunteers comb the pristine beaches of the Maldives, collecting HDPE bottle caps and plastic waste that threatens our marine ecosystem.

500+ Caps per cleanup
Weekly Collection drives
Step 2

Thorough Cleaning

Each piece undergoes meticulous hand-cleaning to remove salt, sand, and ocean residue. We sort by color and plastic type, ensuring only the highest quality materials proceed to the next stage.

5 Cleaning stages
100% Quality rate
Step 3

Precision Shredding

Clean caps are carefully shredded into uniform flakes. This crucial step increases surface area and ensures even melting during the crafting process, creating the foundation for beautiful new products.

3mm Flake size
20:1 Volume reduction
Step 4

Artisan Crafting

Using innovative low-tech methods including repurposed panini presses and custom-made cookie cutters, we transform plastic flakes into beautiful marine-inspired shapes with love and precision.

180°C Melting temp
12 Unique designs
Step 5

Ocean's Gift Reborn

What was once ocean waste is now a treasured piece of jewelry or keychain, carrying a powerful story of transformation. Each piece represents hope, conservation, and the beauty that emerges from conscious action.
